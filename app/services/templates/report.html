Salt Remedy

  • Absorbs negative energy and unwanted vibrations.

Light and Fire Element
  • Light a ghee lamp every evening in prayer or entrance area.
  • Activates the Fire Element (Agni Tattva).
  • Encourages positivity, prosperity, and spiritual growth.
Clean Corners
  • Keep corners clean, dry, and well-lit.
  • Dark and cluttered corners attract stagnant energy.
  • Improves energy circulation in the home.
Clocks & Calendars
  • Place clocks and calendars on North or East walls.
  • Supports growth, progress, and opportunities.
  • Avoid placing clocks on the South wall.
